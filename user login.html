About company

Bayerische Motoren Werke AG. commonly reffered to as BMW, is a German multinational corporate manufacture of luxury vehicles & motorcycles headquartered in Munich, Bavaria, Germany.The company was founded in 1916 as a manufactureof aircraft engines , which it produced from 1917 until 1918 and again from 1933 to 1945.
